About Me

I am an MSc student in Computer Science at the University of São Paulo (IME-USP), advised by Prof. Nina S. T. Hirata. My research lies at the intersection of Large Language Models, Geometric Deep Learning, and AI for Science.

Previously, I was a research intern at IBM Research, working on geometric deep learning for materials science with Dr. Mathias Steiner’s team. I was also a research intern at the University of Texas at Austin, hosted by Prof. Zhangyang “Atlas” Wang (VITA Group), where I worked closely with Prof. Junyuan “Jason” Hong on Large Language Models. I received my BSc in Computer Science from IME-USP.
